Anand Negi

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Anand is a visionary leader with over 15 years of experience in enterprise technology, financial management, and digital transformation. As CEO of NAMA Integrated Solutions, he leads a team of technical professionals to assist businesses with complex technology transitions and organizational change management.

Tier-1 Software Expertise: Former CFO at Sage Software Middle East (LSE-listed market leader in SME business solutions), where he drove financial excellence, operational transformation, and growth across the region.
Proven Track Record: Successfully executed enterprise integrations, managed multi-country operations, and led high-performing teams through transformational change across finance, operations, and technology implementations.

Anand's expertise spans strategic planning, change management, and value creation. He is passionate about leveraging emerging technologies—ERP, CRM, HRMS, and beyond—to solve complex business challenges and position organizations for sustainable growth in an evolving technological landscape.
